# AI Content & Social Media - Service Template

## Overview
Monthly social media management using AI-generated content. 8-12 posts/month across platforms.

---

## Package

### Monthly Service ($197/mo)
- 8-12 AI-generated posts
- Platform optimization (Facebook, Instagram, Google Business)
- Auto-posting scheduler
- Engagement response templates
- Monthly performance report

---

## Process

### 1. Discovery
- What platforms do you want to focus on?
- Who is your ideal customer?
- What tone should we use?
- Any promotions/events coming up?

### 2. Content Generation
Each week, AI generates:
- 2-3 educational posts
- 2-3 promotional posts
- 1-2 engagement posts (polls, questions)
- 1-2 behind-the-scenes posts

### 3. Approval
- Client reviews content before posting
- 1 hour turnaround allowed
- Edits included

### 4. Posting
- Schedule posts for optimal times
- Auto-post via Buffer/Hootsuite/Publer

### 5. Engagement
- Monitor comments
- AI-generated response suggestions
- Human reviews before sending

## AI Prompt Setup

### For Plumbers/HVAC
```
Generate a social media post for a plumbing business. 
Tone: Friendly, helpful, professional.
Audience: Homeowners, property managers.
Length: Short (under 100 words).
Include: Practical tip or common issue.
Always end with a call-to-action.
```

### For Salons
```
Generate a social media post for a hair salon.
Tone: Chic, inviting, trendy.
Audience: Women 25-55, beauty enthusiasts.
Length: Short with emoji.
Include: Style inspiration or tip.
End with booking CTA.
```

### For Restaurants
```
Generate a social media post for a restaurant.
Tone: Warm, appetizing, welcoming.
Audience: Local diners, foodies.
Length: Short, descriptive.
Include: Dish highlight or special.
End with reservation CTA.
```
