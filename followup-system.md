## Part 1: AI Email Responses

### Tool: Claude or ChatGPT + Email

### Setup
1. Connect Gmail via IMAP or API
2. Set up filters for common inquiries
3. Create AI prompt for responses

### Common Inquiries + Responses

**"What are your hours?"**
```
Thanks for reaching out! We're open Mon-Fri 8am-6pm, Sat 9am-2pm. Book online anytime: [link]
```

**"How much does [service] cost?"**
```
Great question! Our [service] starts at $[price]. It includes [features]. Would you like a detailed quote?
```

**"Do you serve [area]?"**
```
Yes! We serve [City] and all surrounding areas. Book here: [link] or call [number]
```

**"When can you come?"**
```
We have availability this week! Book here: [link] or call [number]
```

## Part 2: Review Request Automation

### Trigger: Post-Service
After job completion → Send review request

### Sequence
**Email 1: Same day**
```
Subject: Thanks for choosing [Business]!
Body:
Hi [Name],

Thank you for trusting us with your [service] today!

We'd love to hear about your experience. It only takes 30 seconds:
[Google Review Link]

If you have any feedback, please reply - we're always improving.

Best,
[Business Team]
```

**Email 2: If no review after 3 days**
```
Subject: Quick reminder - Share your experience
Body:
Hi [Name],

Just a gentle reminder - we'd really appreciate 30 seconds of your time to leave a review.

It helps other customers make decisions and helps us improve.

[Google Review Link]

Thanks!
```

**Email 3: If no review after 7 days**
```
Subject: We'd love to hear from you
Body:
Hi [Name],

No pressure, but we really value your feedback. Even a quick "Great service!" helps.

Leave a review here: [link]

Or reply with any concerns - we'd love to make things right if anything was missing.

Best
```

## Part 3: Re-Engagement Sequences

### For Dormant Customers
Trigger: No purchase in X months

### Sequence
**Email 1: "We miss you" (Day 0)**
```
Subject: We miss you, [Name]!
Body:
Hi [Name],

It's been a while since we saw you! We hope everything is going well.

Since your last visit, we've added [new service/promo].

Book here: [link]

Hope to see you soon!
```

**Email 2: Special offer (Day 7)**
```
Subject: Special offer just for you
Body:
Hi [Name],

As a valued past customer, we wanted to offer you 20% off your next service.

Use code: WELCOME20

Book here: [link]

Valid for the next 14 days!
```

**Email 3: Final (Day 14)**
```
Subject: Last chance for your special offer
Body:
Hi [Name],

This is your final reminder - your 20% off offer expires in 3 days!

Book here: [link]

See you soon!
```
